Core Focus Areas

HEPEG's research spans a wide range of health economics topics. Our team conducts rigorous analyses across these domains, contributing to evidence-based policy decisions.

Pharmaceutical Policy

Pricing regulation, market access, and the economic evaluation of medicines and health technologies.

Health Insurance Reform

Analysis of coverage models, financing mechanisms, and their effects on access and outcomes.

Health Disparities

Understanding and addressing inequalities in health outcomes across populations, including migrants and underserved communities.

Public Health Interventions

Evaluating the effectiveness and cost-effectiveness of vaccination programs, screening, and preventive measures.

Value-Based Payment

Designing and assessing payment models that link reimbursement to quality and outcomes.

Health Services Research

Studying the organization, delivery, and financing of healthcare services to improve system performance.
