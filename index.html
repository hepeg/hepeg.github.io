About HEPEG

Who We Are

HEPEG's team is comprised of economists, statisticians, and health services researchers based primarily at the University of Verona, with affiliations at Oxford, Ca' Foscari Venice, Insubria, and other European institutions.

We organize and highlight events such as workshops, conferences and seminars. We are involved in a number of cutting-edge, long-term projects funded at the European and national level.

Our work is driven by the belief that sound economic analysis is essential for designing health policies that are effective, equitable, and sustainable.
