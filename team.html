People

Our Team

An interdisciplinary group of economists, statisticians, and health services researchers.

PB

Paola Bertoli

Full Professor, University of Verona

Research interests in Health Economics, Public Economics and Political Economy. Previously at Prague University of Economics and Business and Charles University.

LB

Luigi Boggian

Research Fellow, University of Verona

Joined the Horizon Europe FLASH project in 2023. Research focuses on healthcare inequalities between immigrants and natives in Europe, including forgone care and mental health.

AB

Alessandro Bucciol

Professor, University of Verona & Netspar Fellow

Coordinates the PhD program in Economics and Finance. Research on household savings, public and environmental policy impact, and the economics of dishonesty.

GD

Giulia De Matteis

Jr Research Fellow, University of Verona

Works on the Horizon Europe FLASH project. MSc in Health Economics from University of York and MA in Economics from University of Padua.

MM

Maurizio Malpede

Assistant Professor, University of Verona & EIEE Fellow

Research on historical roots of development and the impacts of climate change and resource extraction in developing countries. PhD from Cattolica and Bicocca University.

EM

Eleonora Matteazzi

Assistant Professor, University of Verona

Research in household behavior and family economics, time allocation, labor supply, welfare economics, and gender discrimination in the labor market.

GM

Giulia Montresor

Postdoc, University of Verona

Previously postdoc at University of Mannheim. Research in Labor Economics, Health Economics, Migration and Development Economics.

CN

Catia Nicodemo

Associate Prof., University of Verona & Oxford Senior Fellow

Senior Research Fellow at Nuffield Department of Primary Care Health Science, University of Oxford. Research in Applied Micro-Econometrics, Labor and Health Economics, Big Data.

CO

Cristina Elisa Orso

Assistant Professor, University of Insubria

Member of the Scientific Committee at Ca' Foscari CARE. Research in health economics, migration, gender, and economics of ageing.

LS

Lucia Schiavon

Postdoc, Cà Foscari Venice & CHILD Fellow

Research fellow at CHILD – Collegio Carlo Alberto and member of the Impact Evaluation Unit. Research interests in Policy Evaluation, Health, Education and Family Economics.

KS

Khalid W.A. Shomali

Postdoc, University of Verona

PhD in Economics from University of Perugia. Visiting researcher at CRES, Universitat Pompeu Fabra. Research in Health Economics, Migration and Labour Economics.
